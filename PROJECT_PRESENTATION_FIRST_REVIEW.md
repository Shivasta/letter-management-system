## SLIDE 6: IDENTIFIED PROBLEM STATEMENT

Existing leave management systems in educational institutions are not able to process requests efficiently due to manual paper-based workflows and lack of automated multi-tier approval tracking and real-time status visibility.

This results in delayed processing, lost paperwork, lack of transparency, and poor administrative efficiency.

Hence, an innovative digital leave management solution is required to automate the approval workflow, enable real-time tracking, and improve institutional efficiency.
